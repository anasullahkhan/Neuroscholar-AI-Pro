Methodology Sample preparation A postnatal neural dissociation kit (Miltenyi, 130-092-628) was used to prepare a single cell suspension from mouse retinas. Cells were centrifuged at 150 xg for 5 min at 4°C. The digested tissue was resuspended in 100 μL of 4% FBS in PBS containing an FITC antibody to CD11b (1:100; BioLegend, 101206) and incubated for 20 min on ice. The cells were washed and suspended with 1 ml of 4% FBS/PBS containing DAPI (1:2000; Thermo Fisher Scientific, 62248).
